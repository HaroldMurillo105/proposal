MARINDUQUE

Marinduque is an island province in the Luzon island group of the Philippines, east of Mindoro, south of Quezon Province and north of Romblon. The province , which is shaped like a heart, is often called " the heart of the Philippines" and is the home of the Moriones Festival held during Holy Week. The island comprises six municipalities which are Boac, Mogpog, Gasan, Buenavista, Torrijos and Sta.Cruz Other than these towns, it also has offshoreislands that include Tres Reyes Islands, Elephant Island, Maniwaya Island and Polo Island
